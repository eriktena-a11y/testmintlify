---
title: "Outlook"
---

Connect your Outlook account to give Hazel access to your calendar events, emails, and the ability to draft responses to your contacts.

<Note>
  Hazel does not send email on your behalf. Every email draft that Hazel creates requires a human in the loop to review prior to sending
</Note>

---

### **Email**

Hazel provides pre-drafted responses for emails that are received from contacts in your CRM. Hazel also uses these emails as context to help you prepare for meetings or leverage in Ask Hazel.

### Calendar

Hazel views your calendar and automatically scheduled note takers to join. Hazel reviews past and future events and provides you with agendas for each event.

[img]

**Permissions to review and accept:**

<Note>
  - View events on all your calendars
  - Read, compose, and send emails from your Outlook account
  - See your primary Google Account email address
  - Associate you with your personal info on Google
</Note>
