### Inviting Team Members

<Frame>
  ![](/images/admin.gif)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Hazel does not import existing roles and permissions from external data sources. Please review this before adding team members. [Learn more about editing household permissions.](https://hazelai.notion.site/2620935ca9a98049b59dc2d930bf408f?pvs=25#2620935ca9a98051bafce3fb2ef74935)
</Warning>

Hazel makes it easy to bring your team onboard. To invite a new member:

1. Go to Settings → Members.
2. Enter the team member’s email address.
3. Select the appropriate role (Owner, Advisor, or Assistant).
4. Click Invite Member.

<Frame>
  ![](https://hazelai.notion.site/image/attachment%3A3303cb50-6fda-4db7-aca8-f10224af6b79%3Aimage.png?table=block&id=2620935c-a9a9-807e-a7bf-c07bcecb4c01&spaceId=a770935c-a9a9-8126-80d0-00036e93da41&width=1420&userId=&cache=v2)
</Frame>

Your teammate will receive an email with instructions to join Hazel. Once they accept, they’ll have access according to the role you selected.

<Info>
  Only Owners can invite new members and manage roles.
</Info>

### Editing Member Roles and Status

You can easily update roles, adjust member status, or manage pending invites from the Members table.

To make changes:

1. Go to Settings → Members.
2. Find the team member you’d like to update.
3. Use the action menu to:
   - Change Role (Owner, Advisor, or Assistant)
   - Set status to Active or Inactive
   - Revoke Invite if the member has not yet joined

<Frame>
  ![](https://hazelai.notion.site/image/attachment%3Af6bb7c92-9101-451b-99c2-d11267637813%3Aimage.png?table=block&id=2620935c-a9a9-804a-b102-d17d8e7a0455&spaceId=a770935c-a9a9-8126-80d0-00036e93da41&width=1420&userId=&cache=v2)

Changes take effect immediately and update the member’s permissions based on their role.

<Info>
  Only Owners can edit roles, manage status, or revoke invites.
</Info>

### Editing Household Permissions

Hazel gives Owners control over who can see specific Households and their related client information.

To edit Household permissions:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to Contacts.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select the Household you’d like to update.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the Edit icon next to the Household name.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Scroll down to the Household Permissions section.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose one of the following visibility options:

    - All – Visible to all users in the firm
    - Advisors – Visible only to Owners and Advisors
    - Private – Visible only to Owners
  </Step>
</Steps>
