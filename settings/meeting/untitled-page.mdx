### Managing Save Videos and Transcripts

You can configure meeting recordings and transcripts in Settings / Meeting Settings. Videos and transcripts are saved by default. If Save Videos or Save Transcripts are toggled off, then updates will be applied to all future meetings.

[img]

### Auto-Schedule Notetakers

Notetakers are automatically added to new calendar events by default. You can disable Auto-schedule Notetakers by toggling off ‘Auto-schedule Notetakers’ in Settings / Meeting Settings. Disabling this will cancel all your existing auto-scheduled notetakers for future meetings.

[img]

### Updating the Notetaker’s Name

You can configure the name of the notetaker joining your meetings in Settings / Meeting Settings / Bot Name. By default, the notetaker will be names ‘Hazel Notetaker’. Any changes made will be applied to all future meetings.

[img]

### Adjusting the Scheduled Join Time

You can configure how many minutes before the meeting the bot should by adjusting in Settings / Meeting Settings / Bot Join Minutes Ahead. By default, the notetaker will join your meetings 1 minute ahead of the scheduled meeting time. You can adjust this as early as 10 minutes before the scheduled meeting time.
