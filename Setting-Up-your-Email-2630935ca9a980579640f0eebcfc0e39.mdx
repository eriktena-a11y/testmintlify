## Add An Email Signature

Head to Settings → Account

<Frame>
![](https://hazelai.notion.site/image/attachment%3A92eef89b-eaf2-4596-ad0c-a74e64aeffc2%3Aimage.png?table=block&id=2630935c-a9a9-8033-a3dc-f49e799021b2&spaceId=a770935c-a9a9-8126-80d0-00036e93da41&width=1420&userId=&cache=v2)

Scroll to HTML Email Signature and add your email signature.

### Turning your Email Signature into HTML text

<Frame>
![](https://hazelai.notion.site/image/attachment%3A15657d50-baa3-4445-a6e9-506389ca6a0c%3Aimage.png?table=block&id=26f0935c-a9a9-80ef-910b-c25bf99133f3&spaceId=a770935c-a9a9-8126-80d0-00036e93da41&width=1420&userId=&cache=v2)

Is your email not copying over as it should? It’s likely because it’s not HTML text. You can leverage Hazel’s chat to turn your non-HTML signature into HTML text. Here’s how:

Copy your email signature + instructions into Hazel “turn the email signature in the image attached into HTML text”
