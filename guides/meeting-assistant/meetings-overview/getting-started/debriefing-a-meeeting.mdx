[img]

1. **Review and sync notes to the CRM** – After each meeting, Hazel generates notes for you. Review these notes and sync them to your CRM to keep it up to date. These notes also power important features like the pre-meeting agenda and chat.
2. **Send meeting summary email to clients** – Hazel creates a meeting summary email for you. Simply review it, make any adjustments if needed, and send it to your clients to provide them with a clear recap.
3. **Review and sync tasks to the CRM** – Hazel generates relevant tasks based on the meeting discussion. Sync these tasks into your CRM so you can stay on top of the most important priorities moving forward.
