---
title: "Ignoring Meetings"
---

<Accordion title="Ignore Meetings Individually">
  When in the Overview or Meetings view, there is an option to ignore meetings one a one-off basis. Click on the menu icon for the meeting and click ‘Ignore Meeting.’

  [img]
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Ignore Meetings En Masse">
  Head to **Settings** → **Meeting Settings**

  Click on ‘Add Meeting Type’ and toggle the ‘Ignore Meeting Type’ indicator. Then add the phrases you would like to Ignore going forward

  [img]
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Restoring Ignored Meetings">
  To restore ignored meetings, head to the Meetings view, navigate to the ‘Ignored’ tab, and update the **Meeting Type** of the meeting to a non-ignored meeting type or ‘Unignore’.

  [img]
</Accordion>
