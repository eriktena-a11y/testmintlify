Hazel is a trustworthy, intelligent AI engine built by Altruist. Hazel connects to your data sources to deliver deeper reasoning and analysis grounded in your practice’s most relevant context.

  ### Ask Hazel

  Built to answer questions, surface insights, and help you get things done. Instead of clicking through endless pages, ask and access in seconds. It’s your intelligent partner that uses your data as the context to analyze and surface what matters most.

  ![Overviewaskhazel](/images/overviewaskhazel.gif)

  <Card icon="message" horizontal="false" href="/Ask-Hazel-2620935ca9a9809d9646dc89ec4fbf09" cta="Learn more about Ask Hazel">
    
  </Card>
  ### Meetings

  Upcoming meetings are displayed with an agenda summary accessible in one click. That way, you’re always prepared to walk in (or log in) confident, informed, and ready to engage.

  <Card icon="calendar" horizontal="false" href="/guides/meeting-assistant/meetings-overview/how-it-works" cta="Learn more about Meetings">
    
  </Card>
  ### Emails

  Your most important client emails are highlighted so you never miss a critical message. Only emails from contacts in your CRM are pulled into Hazel, eliminating unwanted distractions.

  Hazel will then pull relevant contact details to help you draft intelligent responses, ensuring every reply is timely and informed.
