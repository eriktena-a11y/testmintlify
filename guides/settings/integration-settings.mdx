## Managing Integrations

Connect Hazel with your CRM, email, calendar and more. If you didn’t connect during the onboarding flow or would like to check on your connections, simply head to **Settings → Integrations** to manage your integrations.

<Frame>
  ![Managingintegrations](/images/managingintegrations.gif)
</Frame>

## Integration Status

Once you go through the permissions and set up your integrations, there will be a green dot indicating you are **Connected.**

You can disconnect your integrations by clicking on the menu icon, then **Disconnect.**

You can refresh your CRM connection by clicking on **Trigger Sync.**
