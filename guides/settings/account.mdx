The **Account tab** in Hazel’s Settings allows each user to manage their personal profile and basic account details.

### **Personal Information**

- **Profile Photo:** Upload or update your photo using the Upload photo button.
- **Name:** Edit your First name and Last name fields directly.

### **Firm Information**

- **Firm Logo:** Upload or update your logo using the Change logo button.
- **Firm Name:** Edit your Firm Name fields directly.

### **HTML Email Signature**

- You can enter your email signature so it’s populated on emails drafted by the email assistant, and those drafted during the pre-meeting and follow-up emails flows.

  [More details on how to edit your HTML Email Signature](/guides/email-assistant/setting-up-your-email)
