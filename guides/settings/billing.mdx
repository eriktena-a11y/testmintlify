## Managing Subscriptions

Users with the ‘Owner’ role can manage their firms Billing details and update credit card details within **Settings → Billing**. Here’s a breakdown on everything you need to manage your Hazel subscription:

### Add a Credit Card

To add a credit card, go to **Settings → Billing**, then enter your credit card details or connect your [**Link**](https://link.com/) account. Once you click save, your subscription will be added and set to a **Monthly** billing cadence.

### Add new members to Subscriptions

To add new subscriptions, simply go to the members page and add the desired members. Billing will be updated based on the role assigned to each active user.

![Billingaddnewmembers](/images/billingaddnewmembers.png)

### Unsubscribe or Removing existing subscriptions

To unsubscribe or remove a seat from your billing schedule, reach out to [support@hazel.ai](mailto:support@hazel.ai).

<Note>
  Only ‘Owners’ have the ability to manage existing members roles and permissions.
</Note>

### Setting the Billing Schedule

![Billingsetbillingschedule](/images/billingsetbillingschedule.png)

To adjust your billing schedule, select between our Monthly and Yearly options. Pricing will adjust accordingly.

<Note>
  Users save two months by paying annually instead of monthly.
</Note>
